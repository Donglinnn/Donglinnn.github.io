自我介紹 Introduction

大家好，我是胡哲綸，彰化縣員林市人，從小對地圖有著相當的興趣，幼兒園便會使用"Papago"在地圖中亂逛，小學及國高中時會自行創作平面地圖，直到現在也常常開著Google map環遊世界各個角落。
我另一項興趣是鋼琴，在我國小一二年級的時候，我開始了學習鋼琴的路，但國小四年級時放棄了鋼琴，直到國中才再度的彈起了鋼琴直到現在。我喜歡聽古典樂，尤其是蕭邦、李斯特和拉赫曼尼諾夫大師的作品。
學習語言也是我很有興趣的一環，截至目前，依專精程度高低我已學習過英文、日文、法文及俄文等語言，我認為學習語言有助於我更加了解各國的文化及歷史背景。
還有平時的消遣是騎車出去兜風，看看花蓮的美景以及玩電腦遊戲，主要玩英雄聯盟。
大學時入坑了Hololive，目前主推角卷綿芽和沙花叉克羅耶。


I was born in Yuanlin City, Changhua County, ROC. When I was in kindengarten, I used the map in "Papago" application to travel around. I also create 2D map in elementary school and high school. Nowadays, I often use Google map to travel around the world.
I am also interested in piano. When I was around first or second grade in elementary school, I started to learn playing piano, but I abandoned when I was fourth grade. Until seventh grade, I resumed to play piano again. I like to listen classical music, espacially the masterpieces of Mr.Chopin, Liszt, and Rachmaninoff.
Learning languages is also one of my interest, I have learned English, Japanese, French and Russian so far. I think learning language is helpful for understanding the culture and history background of countries.
I like to ride my scooter travel around Hualien and play computer games, espacially League of Legends.
I started to watch Hololive since 2020, BTW.

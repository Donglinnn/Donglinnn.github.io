Introduction

My name is Hanson "Che-Lun" Hu (胡哲綸), I am currently a junior studing in the department of computer science and imformation engineering in National Donghwa University.

I was born in Yuanlin City, Changhua County, ROC. I love to draw and watch 2D maps. Nowadays, I often use Google map to travel around the world.

I am also interested in piano. When I was around first or second grade in elementary school, I started to learn playing piano, but I abandoned when I was fourth grade. Until seventh grade, I resumed to play piano again. I like to listen classical music, espacially the masterpieces of Mr.Chopin, Liszt, and Rachmaninoff.

Learning languages is also one of my interest, I have learned English, Japanese, French and Russian so far. I think learning language is helpful for understanding the culture and history background of countries.

I like to ride my scooter travel around Hualien and I also like to play computer games, espacially League of Legends.

I started to watch Hololive since 2020, my favorite two Vtubers are Tsunomaki Watame and Sakamata Chloe.
